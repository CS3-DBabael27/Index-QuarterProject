PHILLIPINES SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL - CARAGA REGION CAMPUS

EVENTS AND ACTIVITIES

Source:https://crc.pshs.edu.ph/

The Phillipines Science High School Carag Region Campus has been around for almost a decade. With its upbringing came with there own set of sports, Activities, Contests to partake and much more, All in order to bring fun into the hearts of their students. The activites the students partake into are very productive and interactive as even the teachers take high gratitude into the activites by acting upon themselves and granting free points on the designated subject the activity falls into. The games also bring up the "challenging" spirit onto the Phillipines Science High School(PSHS) scholars. These also grant them oppurtinities to develop and enjoy their time in the campus as the sports and contests within the school give the students something to look upon to every day.
