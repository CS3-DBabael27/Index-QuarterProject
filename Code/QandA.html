QUESTION AND ANSWER

Q:What Are the Benefits of being a Pisay Scholar?

A:The Pisay crc campus Provide the following:Free tuition fee, free loan of textbooks, monthly stipend, Uniform/transportation/living allowances for low income groups, dormitory accommodation (if applicable).

Q:What makes pisay crc different from other schools in the Philippines?

A:Pisay is kinda the Science High School. They all provide the highest quality secondary education public schools can give but Pisay is reknowned for the excellence of its students. The curriculum is rigorous and they provide preparatory tertiary education. They come out of there ready to crush the college system.

Q:Is it worth to study in pisay?

A:Yes: We get to study for free, and Pisay gives us a monthly stipend ranging from P500 for those from well-off families to P4,000 for those from the lower socioeconomic brackets. The quality of education that Pisay offers is also unparalleled, especially in terms of science and technology (S&T).

Q:What kind of school is pisay?

A:The Philippine Science High School System is a research-oriented and specialized public high school system in the Philippines that operates as an attached agency of the Philippine Department of Science and Technology.
